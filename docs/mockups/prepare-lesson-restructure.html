What changes. The chapter list, period stepper, and the Prepare CTA are the only things in the main column — the teacher's eye goes straight down them. Suggestion and Your year (committed / proposed / left) move to a right rail on desktop, present but subordinate. On a phone there is no room for a rail, so both collapse into a single context strip between periods and the CTA: one line for the suggestion, a thin two-tone budget bar with a Budget ⌄ toggle that expands to the full legend. Nothing is removed — the same numbers, the same "use" and breakdown actions — it's re-ranked so the decision (which chapter, how many periods, generate) leads and the context follows.

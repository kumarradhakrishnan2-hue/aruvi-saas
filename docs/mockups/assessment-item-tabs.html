Q1.
Learning outcome

Distinguishes the young ones of familiar animals using picture cues.

Question typeMultiple choice question
Cognitive demandRecall

Look at the picture on page 14. Which of these is the duckling?

  • AThe bird with the red comb
  • BThe small bird swimming behind the duck
  • CThe bird sitting on the eggs
  • DThe bird that says cluck
Correct answer

B ✓ — the small bird swimming behind the duck.

What each choice reveals
AConfuses the hen with the duck family.
CReads “young one” as “mother with eggs”.
DMatches by sound, not the picture cue.

Let shy readers answer by pointing at the illustration; name the birds in the home language first if needed.

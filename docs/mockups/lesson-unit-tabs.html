Duck, Hen and the Stream
1.Picture Walk: Duck, Hen and the Stream
Unit 1 of 3
SpineReading
Time35 mins
PedagogyPicture-reading
Teacher notes

Watch for students who confuse ducklings and chicks — use the illustrations to distinguish them before the guided reading begins.

  • Textbook, pp. 12–15 (stream illustrations)
  • Picture cards — duck, hen, duckling, chick
  • Word strips: “duckling” / “chick”
  • Board and chalk
5min

Settling and recap — recall the pond scene from the previous unit.

10min

Picture walk — walk through the illustrations; students predict the story.

10min

Guided reading — read aloud, pausing at each picture-cue word.

8min

Pair retell using the picture cards.

2min

Wrap-up.

Homework

Draw the stream and label the duck and the hen.

Assessment · this unit
Q1Show two picture cards — ask each pair to name which is the duckling and which is the chick.
Q2Ask: where does the stream go? Accept any answer grounded in the illustrations.
